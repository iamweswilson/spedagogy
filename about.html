Meet Our Team

Changing the way specially designed services are being delivered in schools.

Jasmine Shojinaga
Program Developer & Consultant
Jasmine has worked as a Speech-Language Pathologist, trainer and program developer in schools across all grade levels, as well as the medical and outpatient models. Working with hundreds of professionals and graduate students across the United States and Australia gave her a different perspective on providing services in the education setting.
Jenna Gordon
Researcher & Program Development
Jenna, a pediatric Occupational Therapist, specializes in working with children and families within outpatient clinical settings. In addition to direct clinical treatment, Jenna has directed qualitative and quantitative studies to support clinic review and program development with the support of Social Ventures Partner (SVP) to develop infrastructure within non-profit organizations.
Catherine Wilson
Program Developer & Consultant
Catherine is an educator, consultant, presenter, and educational coach. Through her national work with educators and schools on improving their inclusive practices, she has become known for her expertise on meeting the needs of all students through specially designed instruction, appropriate access, and supports for quality outcomes.
